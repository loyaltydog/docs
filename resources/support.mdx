## Direct support

- **Email** — [support@loyalty.dog](mailto:support@loyalty.dog) for technical or account questions.
- **Contact form** — [loyalty.dog/contact-us](https://loyalty.dog/contact-us) for sales, partnerships, or general inquiries.

## What to include in a bug report

To get to a resolution fastest, include:

- The endpoint URL and HTTP method.
- The request body (with secrets redacted).
- The response status and body.
- A timestamp in UTC.
- The `programId` and (where relevant) the `customerId` involved.

## Status

For real-time platform status, see [status.loyalty.dog](https://status.loyalty.dog).

## Security

For security disclosures, email [security@loyalty.dog](mailto:security@loyalty.dog). Please do not file public issues for security-sensitive reports.
